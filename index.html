Food Waste Reduction Platform

By: Team 234 123

Food waste is a pressing issue that contributes to environmental degradation, economic loss, and social inequity. This project, titled “Save That Gravy!”, aims to tackle food waste in the catering industry by leveraging Machine Learning (ML) for predictive analytics and incorporating surplus management solutions to optimize resource utilization.

The platform features a diner prediction model that estimates the expected number of attendees for various event types such as weddings, social gatherings, and corporate events.

Key functionalities include:

  • Machine Learning Models
  • User Profile Management
  • Automated Notifications
  • Surplus management system

Predictive Model For Heart Disease

By: Team 456 264

Cardiovascular disease (CVD) remains a formidable global health challenge, demanding early detection strategies to mitigate its potentially fatal consequences. This study addresses the urgent need for precise predictive models to identify individuals at risk of heart disease.

Our findings underscore the remarkable performance of SVM, achieving an impressive accuracy of 97.53%, with sensitivity and specificity rates of 97.50% and 94.94%, respectively, in heart disease detection. Furthermore, our real-time patient monitoring system, driven by KNN, facilitates continuous parameter tracking and immediate doctor notification via GSM technology upon critical threshold breaches, enhancing proactive intervention strategies

The pie chart illustrates the distribution of different heart disease types. The largest category is Coronary Artery Disease (CAD) at 22.5%, followed by Arrhythmia at 20.2%, Valvular Heart Disease at 19.8%, Cardiomyopathy at 19.0%, and Heart Failure at 18.5%.
